Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 28, 2026

Overview

This Privacy Policy applies to the Android app Magister Virtuālis, package name com.magistervirtualis, presented by Gnipho. Magister Virtuālis is an offline Latin noun declension practice app.

Data Collection

Magister Virtuālis does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data. The app does not use accounts, ads, analytics SDKs, crash-reporting SDKs, tracking SDKs, or network-backed dictionary services.

Permissions

The app does not request Android permissions for location, camera, microphone, contacts, storage, advertising ID, or network access. The practice content is bundled with the app and runs offline on the device.

Local Practice Data

Practice answers, scores, and selected declensions are used only to run the active study session on the device. This session data is not sent to Gnipho or any third party.

Retention and Deletion

Because Magister Virtuālis does not collect personal data on a server, there is no server-side personal data to delete. Local session data can be cleared by ending the session, closing the app, or clearing the app data through Android system settings.

Children

Magister Virtuālis is a general study app and does not knowingly collect personal data from children or any other users.

Changes

If the app changes in a way that affects data handling, this Privacy Policy will be updated before those changes are released.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer through the Magister Virtuālis Google Play listing.
